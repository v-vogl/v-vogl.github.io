Rattled

Personal Project

RPG Prototype

built in Unreal Engine 5

Rattled is a turn-based tactical RPG prototype built in Unreal Engine 5. (inspired by the Heroes of Might and Magic franchise) It has two layers of levels - an overworld with enemy encounters and a battle scene with a hex-grid layout and turn-based combat.
