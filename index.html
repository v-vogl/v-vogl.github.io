Rattled

Turn-Based Tactical RPG Prototype

Unreal Engine 5 C++ / Blueprints Jan 2026 – Now

Rattled is a turn-based tactical RPG prototype built in Unreal Engine 5. (inspired by the Heroes of Might and Magic franchise)

It has two layers of levels - an overworld with enemy encounters and a battle scene with a hex-grid layout and turn-based combat.
